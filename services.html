Our Training Programs

Each of our training programs are tailored to the specific needs of you and your dog. We will work with you to create a unique plan that will fit around your own lifestyle. Training can be done online or an outdoor loaction of your choice.

Good Manners

This is the perfect training if your dog gets overly excited at pretty much anything. This behavior can easily be labeled as disobedience, but the truth is there is just a break down in communication. We’ll focus on the following areas to get things back on track:

Impulse Control

Confidence & Optimism

Calmness

Calmness being the key theme of the program, we’ll teach you how to get your dog to LOVE being calm!


Contact Us For More Info

Freedom Off Leash

Taking your dog for a walk in the park is supposed to be stress free and enjoyable so this is the ideal program for the dogs who tend to take their owners for a walk instead. We focus on the following areas to bring the focus back to you.

Reactivity

Building Calmness

Impulse Control

Focus

The key theme of the program is creating a stronger relationship when outside so that your dog will always choose YOU, over the hundreds of other distractions going on around them!

Puppy Training


Our Puppy Program is designed to teach both dogs and their owners to ensure everyone is set up for success. Our sessions will cover the following:

Getting The Home Prepared

Toilet Training

Biting & Chewing

Basic Recall

Play Time

Enrichmentent

For you, the human, you’ll also learn how to understand body language, the importance of building calmness and the main reasons that typically drive behavioural issues. You’ll also learn how to meet your dogs needs and bring out the very best in them!
