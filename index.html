About Us

For many dog owners, the reality of dog ownership can be somewhat different to the dream. Pulling on the lead, lunging at other dogs or people, barking constantly can become very stressful for the owner. What if I told you it’s also very stressful for the dog. Sometimes, these dogs are labeled aggressive and ‘dominant’, the real reason is they are just WORRIED.

Our goal here at Scotty’s Dogs is to teach you how to communicate with your dog in a way that they understand, we’ll show you how your dog’s mind really works, and how to get the best out of your relationship with them.

Our programs are created specifically to improve communication and build your dog's confidence, optimism and grit. Our training methods are games based using positive techniques only. They are designed to be super fun for you, your dog, and your entire family - and we encourage everyone to get involved in the process!

It’s life changing!
