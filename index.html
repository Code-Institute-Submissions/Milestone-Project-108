About Us

For many dog owners, addressing behavioural issues is a real concern and the reality of dog ownership can be somewhat different to the dream. Walks in the park can become very stressful due to your dog constantly pulling on the lead. Barking at anything and everything, and lunging at other dogs as they pass. Sometimes, these dogs are labeled agressive and ‘dominant’, the real reason is they are WORRIED.


Our goal here at Scotty’s Dogs is to teach you how to communicate with your dog in a way that they understand, we’ll show you how your dog’s mind really works, and how to get the best out of your relationship with them.


Our programs are created specifically to improve communication and build your dog's confidence, optimism and grit. We’ve designed them to be super fun for both you and your dog. And, the entire family can get involved in the process.
